Porsche Taycan Review

An Insight Into Porsche's Trend in The Car World

The Porsche Taycan is a recent model in the Porsche lineup. It was released in 2020, but the brand has been making efforts to make the model a leading figure in its electric model development. As aforementioned, It utilizes a fully electric powertrain. It delivers a high performance driving experience like its popular gasoline counterparts in the brand such as the Porsche GT3 RS. Its dual-motor setup provides instantaneous acceleration and handling that most vehicles previously couldn't scale up to, with the top trim Taycan Turbo S having the capacity to accelerate from 0 to 60 in 2.4 seconds. A unique aspect of the vehicle is its exterior speakers that produce synthetic engine noise. This was done to provide an engaging driving experience despite its quiet electric powertrain. Public data is relevant to the vehicle because of infrastructure mapping. Institutions can utilize the Taycan's high energy requirement to optimize the placement of public power grids and high voltage charging stations.
